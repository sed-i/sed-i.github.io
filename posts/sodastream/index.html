    How much carbonated water do you get from a sodastream?

    March 6, 2022
    review
    sodastream, club-soda

    How does sodastream compare to club soda in terms of pricing?

    I used a sodastream “60L cylinder” (14.5 oz / 410 g / 0.63L CO2) to carbonate small (450 ml) and large (840 ml) bottles, and recorded how many of each I carbonated (three “buzzes” per bottle):

    450 ml 840 ml Total vol.
    1 20 16 22.44 l
    2 9 20 20.85 l

    Assuming I forgot to record a few, let’s say a single sodastream canister carbonates 25 litres of water, which means

    CO2 Soda yield # 222 ml cans # 355 ml cans
    410 g 25 l 113 70

    At the grocery store:

    • club soda is priced at about 1.5-1.8 $/100ml;
    • a CO2 cansiter is priced at about $40, i.e. 0.16 $/100ml of soda.

    So sodastream is:

    • 10 times cheaper than cans (yet more bubbly than cans);
    • significantly reducing your trash volume.
